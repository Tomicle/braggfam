Basic Info

Need-to-Know for Epic Adventures

Check-in Thursday, July 16 at 4:00 PM. Address will be sent via text. Rooms are pre-assigned — look for the sign with your name on the door.

Review the menu below — each family is signed up to provide specific drinks and snacks.

Bring a day pack to carry water and snacks on the family adventures.

The hikes are in red dirt — it stains everything! Bring "red dirt" adventure shoes, shorts, and a tee-shirt you don't mind ruining. Anything old works great.

Games like LRC will require everyone to have an individually wrapped item (gummy bears, candy, gum, nuts) — or bring a bunch of nickels (no pennies; nickels are the lowest denomination).

Every night we'll do a family walk-through and tidy-up time, including a quick bathroom wipe down.
